About us

We are a Ukrainian jewelry manufacturer. Jewelry is made by hand in Kyiv, but we are carried all over the world. We mainly work with recycled silver and gold, we apply the principles of lean production. All our jewelry is first drawn by our designers, and then made by craftsmen.

You won't find jewelry like the ones in our store anywhere else.

Each piece is crafted with inspiration by a specific jeweler: from scratch to finished product. We love what we do, and we approach our work responsibly and ethically. Focusing on our customers, our products highlight the best inside and out.
Usually, the workshop works on pre-orders, but a certain number of pieces are in stock.

Our brand is about sophistication and audacity.

Values

Nature

We strive to package our parcels in an environmentally friendly way. While we can't completely give up all the beauty of packaging, as we believe it plays a crucial role in the gift and the gifting process, we do our best to use paper and cardboard that can be recycled.

Uniqueness

The natural desire for uniqueness, something special and not mass-produced, has brought us all here. All the jewelry is exclusive because it is impossible to create two identical pieces. They are similar, but they differ.

People

We understand when someone asks about a hundred different pieces of jewelry or places an order only after a month. This is completely normal and not irritating at all.
